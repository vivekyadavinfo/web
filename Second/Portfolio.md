## Biography

Dr. Rajiv Raj is a renowned Hindi poet, educator, and motivational speaker based in Etawah, Uttar Pradesh. He has won the prestigious National Teacher Award from the President of India and is well-known for his moving, accessible poetry and stage performances.

## Key Achievements

- National Teacher Award (President of India)
- Prahlad Yaduvanshi Alankaran (2015)
- Gopal Das Neeraj Yuva Geetkar Samman (2018)
- Mahapran Nirala Samman (2018)
- Dr. Vishnu Saxena Geet Samman (2019)
- Kavivar Brajshukla Ghayal Smriti Samman (2019)
- Geet Gangotri Samman (2021)
- Numerous other literary and state honors.

## Notable Literary Works
